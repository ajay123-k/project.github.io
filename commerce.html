About the Department: Faculty of Commerce is a pioneer faculty of this college. June 1983 is the year of establishment of College and of this Faculty. Since the date of establishment till today this is a single largest faculty of this college.

Academic Information: As per present structure of Savitribai Phule Pune University, at UG level three special subjects are taught namely
1) Business Entrepreneurship.
2)Banking and Finance,
3) Costing.

After graduation eager students can pursue their post-graduation in the same college because we have PG Department since 1990. At PG level one Elective subject is available i.e. Business Administration.
